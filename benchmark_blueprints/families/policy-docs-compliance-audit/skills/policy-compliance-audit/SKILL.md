# Policy Compliance Audit

Use this skill when a task requires evidence-backed compliance findings from a frozen policy and runbook corpus.

## Workflow
1. Rank sources by authority before classifying findings.
2. Distinguish policy violation, documentation drift, and compliant exception.
3. Prove every finding from the corpus.
4. Avoid overstating non-authoritative implementation notes.

## Anti-Patterns
- Treating runbooks as higher authority than policy.
- Declaring a violation without a corpus citation.
- Missing a narrow documented exception.

## Done Signal
- Findings are correct, authority-ranked, and evidence-backed.
